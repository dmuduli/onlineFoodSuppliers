About us

Homemade is World’s first and only platform that promotes and sells all kinds of items made at home ranging across Food, Fashion, Décor, Plants & Planters and many more categories coming up.

Homemade platform was conceptualized, designed and created proudly in a home of India in September 2020 right in midst of the lockdown period and the key purpose was to provide opportunity for families to have a second source of income and also for people to have option to get safer, higher quality, hygienic items especially food. Founded by renowned Industry Leaders with global experience of over 20 years in online business, Homemade brings the most diverse range of items that customers can browse or buy in simple clicks.
